About Us

Trading is a 100% black-owned SMME based in Lower Shiloh Village, Whittlesea. We specialize in construction, civil works, plumbing, plant/tool hire, and commercial cleaning services. Founded on reliability and community upliftment, we deliver quality work that meets spec, budget, and deadline. As active members of the Hewu Contractors Forum, we're committed to growing local skills while serving government and private clients across the Eastern Cape.

Services

*Building & Civil- New builds, renovations, concrete work, paving, aprons, ablutions, and structural work. CIDB aligned. Bricklayers on site.

*Plumbing-Domestic & commercial plumbing, leak detection, pipework, geyser installs, ablution facilities. Plumber with tools, bathroom install.

*Plant & Tool Hire- TLB, excavators, tipper trucks, compactors, scaffolding, concrete mixers. Wet & dry rates available. Your TLB/excavator, tipper truck lineup.

*General Service Provider-Labor supply, maintenance contracts, material supply, SHEQ compliance support.Safety file + team toolbox talk.
